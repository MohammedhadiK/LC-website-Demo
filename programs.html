Our Programs

Listening initiatives that hold communities together

Each program is built to nurture empathy, reduce isolation, and strengthen support networks — for individuals, campuses, and organisations alike.

01

Listening Helpline

24/7 phone and chat support staffed by trained volunteer listeners who offer crisis de-escalation, emotional validation, and resource referrals.

  • • Confidential conversations in English & Malayalam
  • • Escalation protocols with licensed partners
  • • Post-call check-ins for high-risk cases
02

Listening Circles

Facilitated small-group spaces hosted in campuses, workplaces, and neighbourhoods for people to share, reflect, and heal together.

  • • Circle anchors supported by LC facilitators
  • • Story prompts that centre empathy
  • • Optional thematic tracks (grief, student stress, caregivers)
03

Listening Labs

Custom trainings and workshops that equip organisations to embed listening culture, from frontline teams to leadership cohorts.

  • • Modules on trauma-informed communication
  • • Role-play and reflective practice sessions
  • • Post-workshop mentoring for champions
04

Campus Listening Clubs

Student-led clubs that cultivate peer support, mental health literacy, and collective care practices across colleges and universities.

  • • Club starter kits & facilitator guides
  • • Monthly supervision with LC mentors
  • • Outreach campaigns co-created with students
05

Community Listening Hubs

Pop-up listening spaces co-designed with local partners to bring accessible support to neighbourhoods facing collective stress.

  • • Partnerships with NGOs & resident groups
  • • On-ground listener booths & art-based prompts
  • • Data insights shared back with communities
06

Fellowship for Emerging Listeners

A 6-month journey that blends immersive training, supervised listening hours, and peer reflection to form the next cadre of LC leaders.

  • • 120 hours of experiential learning
  • • Cross-cohort learning circles
  • • Capstone project in the fellow’s community

How it works

Join a Listening Community pathway

Whether you’re seeking support, wanting to volunteer, or partnering with us, our onboarding journeys make it easy to get started.

Individuals

  1. Reach out via our Helpline or WhatsApp.
  2. Share your needs; we listen and co-create a support plan.
  3. Access circles, referrals, and follow-up care as needed.

Volunteers

  1. Apply to the Listening Skills Training cohort.
  2. Complete immersive training + supervised practicum.
  3. Co-facilitate helpline shifts or circles with mentor support.

Partners

  1. Book a discovery call with our partnerships team.
  2. Co-design a program tailored to your community.
  3. Launch with LC facilitators & track impact together.
