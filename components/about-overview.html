About Us

A community built on care, empathy, and listening.

Who We Are Listening Community (LC) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to active listening and suicide prevention. We believe that healing begins when every person feels heard, understood, and supported.

Our Belief At LC, we believe that every voice matters - and that listening can save lives. By fostering compassion and connection, we aim to build a community where no one is left unheard.

What We Do Our initiatives include a Listening Helpline, Listening Circles, awareness programs, and skill-building workshops that empower people to listen with empathy and support one another. We also engage in advocacy, training, and collaborations to strengthen community-based mental health care.
